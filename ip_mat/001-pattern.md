# IPMAT (Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test) 
It is mainly conducted for 5-year IPM programs of institutes like Indian Institute of Management Indore and Indian Institute of Management Rohtak.

There are different patterns depending on the institute.

# IPMAT Indore Pattern

Conducted by Indian Institute of Management Indore

| Section                             | Questions | Time   |
| ----------------------------------- | --------- | ------ |
| Quantitative Ability (Short Answer) | 15        | 40 min |
| Quantitative Ability (MCQ)          | 30        | 40 min |
| Verbal Ability (MCQ)                | 45        | 40 min |

### Total

* Total Questions: 90
* Total Time: 120 minutes
* Mode: Computer-Based Test (CBT)

### Marking Scheme

* Correct answer: +4
* Wrong MCQ: -1
* Short Answer wrong/unattempted: No negative marks

### Important Point

Indore is considered tougher in Quantitative Aptitude.
Short-answer quant questions make it different from other BBA exams. ([Free Job Alert][1])

# IPMAT Rohtak Pattern

Conducted by Indian Institute of Management Rohtak

| Section              | Questions | Time   |
| -------------------- | --------- | ------ |
| Quantitative Ability | 40        | 40 min |
| Logical Reasoning    | 40        | 40 min |
| Verbal Ability       | 40        | 40 min |

### Total

* Total Questions: 120
* Total Time: 120 minutes
* All are MCQs

### Marking Scheme

* Correct answer: +4
* Wrong answer: -1

### Important Point

Rohtak includes a full Logical Reasoning section, unlike Indore. ([Free Job Alert][1])

# Main Subjects Asked

## Quantitative Aptitude

Topics from Class 9–12 maths:

* Percentages
* Profit & Loss
* Ratio
* Time & Work
* Algebra
* Geometry
* Number System
* Permutation & Combination
* Probability

## Verbal Ability

* Reading Comprehension
* Grammar
* Vocabulary
* Para jumbles
* Sentence correction

## Logical Reasoning (Rohtak mainly)

* Seating arrangement
* Puzzles
* Blood relations
* Coding-decoding
* Series
* Syllogism ([Collegedunia][2])

# Difficulty Level

Typical student opinion:

* IPMAT Indore → harder quant
* IPMAT Rohtak → easier than Indore but speed-based
* JIPMAT → generally easier than both

Reddit discussions from aspirants also commonly mention Indore as the toughest among IPMAT variants. ([Reddit][3])

# Selection Process

Usually:

1. Entrance Exam
2. Shortlisting
3. Personal Interview (PI)

Final admission depends on:

* Exam score
* Interview
* 10th/12th marks (varies by institute)
